Subway

In November 2016, Chengdu completed the revision of the urban rail transit network planning. Among them, the vision recommendation network consists of 46 lines, including 23 general lines, 16 express lines, 3 existing city railway lines, 1 city inner control line line (Jianyang line), and 3 cross-city lines 18- Line 1 (Ziyang Line), No. 39 (Meishan) Line Extension Line, No. 40 (Deyang) Line, the total length is about 2,450km; the long-term line network consists of 34 lines, with a total length of about 1765km.

The 2016 version of the network fully reflects the planning concept of rail transit leading the development of the city, and the planning has achieved global coverage.

Taxi Service

Chengdu Rongcheng Taxi Co., Ltd. is a taxi service company under the Chengdu Public Transport Group. It was established in September 2005. As of March 2018, Chengdu Rongcheng Taxi Co., Ltd. has 1310 state-owned taxi operating rights, including 1,220 ordinary taxi operating rights and 90 special taxi operating rights.

Chengdu taxi body color is green or blue.
