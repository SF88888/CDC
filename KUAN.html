WIDEAND NARROW ALLEY

Brief introduction

It consists of a wide alley, a narrow alley and a well alley. It is an existing Qing Dynasty ancient street in Chengdu. The wide alleys concentrate on the most complete and old buildings in the whole block. Here you can enjoy bowls of tea, eat authentic Sichuan cuisine, and experience the customs of old Chengdu. Narrow Alley has both the buildings of the late Qing Dynasty and the early Republic of China, as well as the early Western-style buildings. It is a casual lifestyle area dominated by Western-style food culture and art leisure. Jingxiangzi is a miniature of the typical folk Chengdu. In addition to the fascinating brick culture wall, it also gathers Chengdu's specialty snacks and folk customs.
